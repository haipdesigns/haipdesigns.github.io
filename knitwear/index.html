Haip focuses on the individuality of the garments and thus the wearer through thoughtful research that translates into handcrafted, minimal and playful garments. As well as using aspects of environmentally conscious design like choosing natural fibers and designing with waste concious patterns.

Knit swatch techniques on Dubied double-bed machine. Inspired by the textures and colors of the East Village.

Knit interpretation of Paul Signac's Pink Clouds. Done on domestic single-bed.
